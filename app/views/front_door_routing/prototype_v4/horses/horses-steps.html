Export horses to United Arab Emirates

Check what you need to do permanently or temporarily export horses to UAE

  1. Step 1 Find an official vet

    You will need an official vet to inspect your horse and confirm it meets the health requirements of the destination country and to sign the export health certificate.

    1. Exporters in England, Scotland and Wales need to find a professional to certify export health certificates.
    2. Exporters in Northern Ireland need to contact DAERA.

  2. Step2 Apply for an export health certificate

    You will need to apply to the Animal and Plant Health Agency for an export health certificate.

    • Find out more about applying for an export health certificate
    • Apply for an export health certificate

  3. Step 3 Check if you need an export welfare declaration

    You may not need an export welfare declaration if your horse is flying direct to UAE.

    • Check if you need an export welfare declaration
