Import orchids from Taiwan to Great Britain

Find out what documents you’ll need and what arrangements you’ll need to make. There are different rules if you are importing to Northern Ireland.

  1. Step 1 Register for Defra trade services

    You’ll need a Government Gateway account to register to use Defra services online.

    1. Register with government gateway

  2. Step 2 Get a phytosanitary certificate

    This is issued by the plant health authority in the country where your supplier is. It shows that the orchids meet the legal requirements for entry into the UK.

    1. Find out more about phytosanitary certificates

  3. Step 3 Check if the species you want to import is endangered

    Find out if the orchids needs a permit under the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES).

    1. Check if you need a CITES permit to import or export endangered species

  4. Step 4 Notify Animal & Plant Health Authority (APHA) about your import

    Tell APHA where, when and how your consignment will enter Great Britain. Arrange an inspection at the UK border.

    • Log a pre-arrival notification
    • Choose a point of entry
    • Get materials inspected at the UK border
