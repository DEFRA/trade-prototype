  1. Step 1 : Register for Defra trade services

    Create a Government Gateway ID to access Defra trade services online.

    1. You are currently viewing: Register for Defra trade services

  2. Step 2 : Get a phytosanitary certificate

    1. You must get a phytosanitary certificate for each consignment from the plant health authority in the country where your supplier is.

  3. Step 3 : Apply for a CITES permit

    Apply for a permit to import/export endangered species. There are additional rules that you must adhere to.

  4. Step 4 : Notify the animal & plant health authority of the import

    This includes:

    • Making arrangements at a the border
    • Logging a pre-notification
    • Paying for any inspections
